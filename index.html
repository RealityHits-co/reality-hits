Reality Hits

The board game where disasters strike and the strong survive.

About the Game

Reality Hits is a board game focused on natural disasters by state. Compete with other players to earn the most money while navigating real-world events. Obstacles await, but so do opportunities. In the end, "Reality always Hits." Will you survive?

Available online until further notice.
