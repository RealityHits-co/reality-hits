Welcome to Reality Hits

Reality Hits is a survival board game where you take on state-themed jobs, face disasters, and compete to come out on top. It's chaotic. It's real. It's fun.

Play Roles:

  • Doctor, Farmer, Fisherman, Lawyer, etc.
  • Every role has disasters and power-ups
  • State-specific events add twists and drama
